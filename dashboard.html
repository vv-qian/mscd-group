Median Gross Rent 2014

A Note

The fire-colored circles represent grocery and liquor stores. Low-end grocery stores ($) are red, higher-end grocery stores ($$) are orange and liquor/corner stores are yellow. Gray circles represent grocery stores with an undetermined price range.

This project is meant to be an exploratory dashboard with much possibilities for future work. A couple of areas for further work: one, include amenities on the map such as museums, parks, cafes and the like and two, rank grocery stores according to customer satisfaction in addition to price range.
